Fortune 22 (Moderately Fortunate)

To clutch onto resentment is to grasp a blade by its edge—it only wounds the one who holds it. If
you carry bitterness toward someone or something in the past, this fortune advises you to let go of
your fixation. Forgiving others is also freeing yourself; only by removing the shackles of grudges
can you move forward lightly. Once old grievances are released, your heart will regain its lightness
and joy. The road ahead will likewise smooth out and brighten, no longer weighed down by
yesterday’s burdens.
