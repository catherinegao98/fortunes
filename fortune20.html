Fortune 20 (Moderately Fortunate)

At life’s crossroads, your heart wavers between two pulls. This choice is about values and true
desire—not as simple as it may first seem. This fortune urges you to heed the call of your inner truth
and not be led astray by outside noise or pressure. Quiet your mind and weigh the decision with
your soul’s sincerity. The choice made with calm deliberation and honest intent will guide you onto
the path that aligns with destiny’s plan, the one meant truly for you.
