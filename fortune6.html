Fortune 6 (Moderately Fortunate)

Health is a matter of balancing opposites—rest and activity in harmonious measure. If your body or
mind has been unwell recently, do not worry overly; this fortune portends recovery on the horizon.
Now is the time to recuperate. Maintain a wholesome daily rhythm, tuning your body and soul back
into resonance like an instrument being delicately adjusted. As morning light chases away the cold
of night, your energy and spirit will gradually refill, and life will return in full bloom.
