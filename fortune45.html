Fortune 45 (Moderately Fortunate)

A breeze whispers through the treetops, and a distant bell softly chimes. This fortune hints that
unseen guidance is all around you now, if you only quiet your mind to hear it. You may catch a hint
of it in a dream, an intuition, or an offhand remark—these are messages the universe is sending you.
Slow your pace and attend to the subtle voice within. In that stillness, understanding will blossom.
You will experience a sudden epiphany, as if a light has been switched on in a dark room, and find
the answer and direction you seek.
