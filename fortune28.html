Fortune 28 (Moderately Fortunate)

Inspiration is a morning breeze that brushes your face and is gone if not seized. You are nurturing a
new idea in your work or creativity; this fortune urges you to keep refining it diligently and do not
grow lax. Your talent needs polishing, as a rough gem must be carefully cut for its brilliance to
shine. Do not let up in your efforts—carve and hone your idea like jade. If you persist, your creation
or project will eventually stand out from the rest, gleaming with excellence and drawing all eyes in
admiration.
