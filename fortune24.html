Fortune 24 (Moderately Fortunate)

Wealth flows like water and must be channelled wisely to endure. This fortune suggests your
finances are stable now, but take care not to spend recklessly. Keep to the proper path and live
within your means—remember that even a slow stream can swell into a great river. In investments,
favour the steady and secure over the quick and risky; do not chase gains that grow too fast. By
maintaining prudence, your treasury will gradually grow ever more abundant, fed by a stream that
never runs dry.
