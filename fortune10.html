Fortune 10 (Very Fortunate)

A fortunate aura crowns your house of prosperity—a golden opportunity is emerging. This fortune
implies the time of reaping after sowing: your prior toil will yield a rich reward. Both steady
earnings and surprise windfalls are likely, though you must act decisively as chances appear and
vanish in a blink. Through practical planning, you can keep wealth flowing steadily, like careful
sowing that produces a bountiful harvest. Stay grounded and wise, and your financial abundance
will be lasting.
