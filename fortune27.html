Fortune 27 (Very Fortunate)

The Hermit’s quiet guidance comes to you; wisdom blooms in stillness. This fortune shows you
stand at the threshold of a spiritual awakening. By withdrawing into solitude, you gain a better view
of the whole. Like the Hermit in the dark holding up his lantern to light the way, the deep questions
in your heart will soon find their answers. Devote yourself to inner reflection and mindful practice
—you will discover the light of wisdom already illuminating your path ahead, bringing a sudden,
enlightening clarity to your life.
