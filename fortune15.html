Fortune 15 (Very Fortunate)

Like the phoenix that bathes in flame and rises anew from ash, so are you on the verge of a rebirth
of fortune. This fortune heralds that after your trials, new good luck is about to descend. Past
hardships have become fuel for your growth; adversity reaches its end and turns to prosperity. Just
as the first light of dawn breaks the long night, driving away darkness, a brand new chapter is
unfolding for you. It brings unexpected joy and rewards—prepare to be pleasantly surprised by the
coming delights.
