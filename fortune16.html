Fortune 16 (Moderately Fortunate)

Like a farmer sowing good seed in spring, your efforts are quietly taking root beneath the soil.
Though your work has yet to blossom, this fortune shows that a solid foundation is already in place.
Continue to tend to your goals without impatience—when the season is ripe, a lush harvest will
naturally arrive. Maintain your patience and faith, for the rewards that grow in the future will
exceed your expectations, abundant beyond what you imagine.
