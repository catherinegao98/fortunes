Fortune 2 (Very Fortunate)

blossoms like spring flowers. Sincere feelings and choices will lead to a blessed union. The
harmony between you and your beloved deepens, as if two stars in the night sky are slowly drawing
together. Simply treat each other with truth and warmth—your fated love will naturally come to
fruition, and you will walk hand in hand under the light of a full moon.
