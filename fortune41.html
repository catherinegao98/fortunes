Fortune 41 (Moderately Fortunate)

A loss may turn out to be a blessing in disguise. This fortune reveals that what seems like a setback
or defeat now will soon give rise to a fortunate new opportunity. In a situation that appears unlucky,
a hidden chance is tucked away. Adjust your perspective and wait calmly for the tides to turn.
Before long, you will find that what you lost returns to you in another form. You’ll look back and be
thankful for the change that once seemed to be misfortune, for it clears the way to unexpected good
fortune.
