Fortune 17 (Moderately Fortunate)

Good intentions are a lamp that can light your way even in the darkest night. This fortune signifies
that your recent acts of kindness and help are accumulating unseen blessings; accordingly, your
relationships will quietly improve. Give someone a rose, and a sweet fragrance clings to your hand.
Your sincere compassion will bring you others’ gratitude and reciprocity in time. Trust that the good
seeds you have planted will yield good fruits. In helping others, your own fortune rises invisibly,
illuminating your path ahead.
