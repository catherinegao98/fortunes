Fortune 42 (Moderately Fortunate)

When all paths seem closed, transformation is the way through. This fortune hints that if you face a
deadlock now, you should step outside the old framework and seek a new approach. Clinging to
outworn routines can only maintain the stalemate; only by carving out a fresh path can you break
through the impasse. Be bold in innovation and try something unorthodox—a fresh idea will bring
about a sudden turn for the better. Solutions will present themselves as soon as you embrace change,
and the obstacles will dissolve with surprising ease.
