Fortune 37 (Very Fortunate)

Heaven’s cupid smiles upon a blessed union; bridal lights cast their rosy glow. This fortune foretells
that a marriage or partnership will bear splendid fruit—you and the one destined for you will join in
joyous union. Though fate brings you together, you must still seize the moment. Through mutual
trust and forbearance, the relationship will grow ever more perfect. A propitious marriage is near at
hand; be neither afraid nor doubtful. Simply follow the call of happiness in your heart, and this
beautiful bond will be fulfilled in all its glory.
