Fortune 40 (Very Fortunate)

Your name travels far on the winds of renown, like a noble tree standing proud in full bloom. This
fortune predicts that your talents and accomplishments will be widely acclaimed. Honours or
awards may soon come your way. At long last, your hard work is arriving at its fruitful conclusion
—you will dazzle in the spotlight before all. Accept the applause and honour with grace, for these
accolades are the rewards you have earned. Your influence will expand along with your fame, laying
the groundwork for even greater successes ahead.
