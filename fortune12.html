Fortune 12 (Moderately Fortunate)

Learning is like rowing upstream: to cease moving forward is to fall behind. Though you feel a
heavy pressure in your studies now, this fortune promises that diligence will earn its reward. Do not
fear the difficulties along the way—Mercury, patron of scholars, watches over those who persevere.
Keep your head bowed in hard study; your talents will break through like spring shoots from the
earth, soon to astonish everyone. With persistence, the seeds of knowledge you sow will blossom
into achievement.
