Fortune 29 (Moderately Fortunate)

Blood runs deep, and family is your eternal safe harbour. If you have been toiling far from home,
remember to reach out to your elders; this fortune suggests that family will bring you strength and
support. Perhaps a relative is quietly keeping you in their thoughts—your hello will be the greatest
comfort to them. In the wisdom passed down through your family lies guidance as well. Sit down
for a heartfelt talk with a parent or elder, and you may receive unexpected insight and counsel that
inspire you.
