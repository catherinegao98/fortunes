Fortune 11 (Moderately Fortunate)

A luminous moon can be obscured by dark clouds; so now the truth remains half-veiled. The
situation that concerns you may appear tangled and confusing, making it hard to see the way. This
fortune warns you not to be deceived by surface illusions—sharpen your inner sight to pierce
through the haze. With keen insight, discern the subtle truths hidden from ordinary eyes. When the
fog lifts, all will be revealed clearly, and you will be able to make a wise and well-informed
decision.
