Fortune 9 (Moderately Fortunate)

Household luck hums at a steady warmth—the hearth is glowing, though it may need a fresh log.
Perhaps there have been small misunderstandings among family members recently, but this fortune
shows that familial bonds will ultimately mend. With an open, forgiving heart and honest
communication, you can expect a time of “peace at home, prosperity in all.” Family members are
like branches and leaves of one tree: after weathering the storm, the tree still bursts into bloom, and
the home again fills with harmony and joy.
