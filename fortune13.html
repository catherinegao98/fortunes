Fortune 13 (Moderately Fortunate)

Love waxes and wanes like the moon, now full, now crescent. You and your beloved may be
experiencing a brief ebb in your romance, but this fortune shows the relationship is not truly
diminishing—only a passing cloud before the moon’s face. If your hearts remain true, be patient
and trust that the moonlight will return in all its glory. Once you both share your hearts openly and
clear up any misunderstandings, the light of your love will shine anew, deeper and steadier than
before.
