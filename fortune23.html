Fortune 23 (Very Fortunate)

“Where mountains rise, carve a road; where rivers run, build a bridge.” This fortune foretells
extremely auspicious luck in travel. On your journey you will gather joyful surprises and rich
insights. Just as The World card in the tarot signifies fulfillment, traveling far and wide will reward
you with wisdom and a greatly broadened spirit. All along your trek, benefactors will accompany
you and splendid vistas will unfold. This journey will add a vibrant stroke to the story of your life, a
chapter of completion and wonder.
