Fortune 49 (Very Fortunate)

A winged messenger approaches with news—joyful tidings are near. This fortune shows that the
message you have long awaited is about to arrive, and it is most likely exhilarating good news. If
you have been waiting on a decision or result—be it a promotion, acceptance, or other outcome—it
is poised to come through in your favour. Fate is accelerating forward in a direction that benefits
you. Soon you will be all smiles, celebrating this triumphant moment with delight, as the long-
anticipated news finally comes and lifts your heart.
