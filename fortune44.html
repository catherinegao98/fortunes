Fortune 44 (Moderately Fortunate)

The heavens bear witness—truth and justice will prevail in the end. If you are entangled in a dispute
or conflict, this fortune shows that fairness lives in the hearts of people, and the truth will eventually
come to light. Do not, in your feeling of grievance, resort to rash or extreme actions. Maintain your
integrity and convince others with reason, showing the honourable character of a true gentle soul.
Keep a clear conscience and stand by your principles; the outcome of this matter will develop in a
way that favours you once all is revealed.
