Fortune 36 (Moderately Fortunate)

What was lost is found; friends long apart reunite. This fortune predicts that small happinesses will
grace your life. Perhaps something you thought gone forever will unexpectedly reappear, or a dear
friend you haven’t seen in years will suddenly reach out to you. These everyday joys are the
universe’s gentle gifts. Keep gratitude in your heart, and you will begin to see surprises and warmth
hiding in every corner of ordinary life. Even in the most unassuming day, a bit of magic and cheer is
waiting for you.
