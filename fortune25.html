Fortune 25 (Moderately Fortunate)

Beneath a calm surface, hidden currents may swirl—still waters run deep. There might be quiet
competition or concealed conflict around you, but this fortune counsels that you need not meet
aggression with aggression. Use stillness to counter motion: by staying composed and steady, your
opponents will find themselves with no target. Conduct yourself openly and with integrity; gossip
and jealousy will exhaust themselves and fade away. Your reputation and position will remain
secure and unharmed, protected by your unwavering fairness and calm.
