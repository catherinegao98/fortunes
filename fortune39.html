Fortune 39 (Moderately Fortunate)

Clouds may momentarily veil the sun, but they will pass. You might be worrying over some
unknown dangers, imagining the worst—but this fortune reveals that these fears are largely
unfounded. Things are not as dire as you think, so do not let anxiety overwhelm your heart. Remain
calm and watch events unfold without panic. By meeting challenges with a steady mind, you will
find your fears proven false. In the end, the rain will pass and the sun will shine again; all will be
well, and you will wonder why you ever doubted.
