Fortune 48 (Moderately Fortunate)

Winter’s chill is ending; spring’s first thunder rolls. A dreary chapter has passed, and this fortune
predicts your situation will gradually brighten. The heavy burdens of yesterday are beginning to lift
—just as after a long cold night, the morning sun now rises and everything starts to turn toward the
light. You persevered through the dark, and you have emerged tougher and wiser. From here
onward, opportunities will arrive on the gentle spring breeze. You will watch the results of your
hard work begin to blossom, one after another, bringing growth and hope.
