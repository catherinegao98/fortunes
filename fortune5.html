Fortune 5 (Moderately Fortunate)

Relationships are like harp strings sounding together—only in harmony can the melody endure. Of
late, minor quarrels may have arisen among friends or colleagues, but this fortune indicates
misunderstandings will melt away. By putting yourself in another’s place and responding with a
generous heart, any rift will dissolve like snow in spring, and the flower of friendship will bloom
anew. Remember that harmony is priceless; sincere communication will deepen your bonds even
more than before.
