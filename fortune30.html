Fortune 30 (Very Fortunate)

With dragon’s roar and tiger’s cry, a leader is crowned. This fortune portends that you will take on
great responsibility and gain the trust and devotion of many. Like an emperor ascending the throne,
you will wield authority and influence in your field. Unfurl your talents and ambitions boldly—your
team will rally behind you, and your enterprise will expand its reach. Your decisiveness and vision
will guide those around you, and together you will climb to new heights. Embrace this coronation
of opportunity and lead with confidence.
