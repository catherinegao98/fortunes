Fortune 4

Fortune 4 (Moderately Fortunate)

As the Fool of the tarot sets out on a new quest, carrying nothing but faith, so do you embark on a
fresh journey. Though the road may be fraught with frost and storm, it also holds hidden wonders
and broad new vistas. Any jostling along the way is no cause for fear. With courage and curiosity in
your heart, the clouds will clear and the moonlight will guide you, gifting you an experience of
profound meaning. Everything you witness and learn on this path will become a precious treasure to
you in days to come.
