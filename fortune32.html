Fortune 32 (Moderately Fortunate)

A hall filled with pearls and jade cannot outshine one light lit in the heart. This fortune reveals that
you must not be dazzled by fame or superficial riches—for true abundance comes from inner
fulfillment. Material success is delightful, yes, but if the spirit is barren, no wealth can buy peace of
mind. Pause in your pursuits and ask yourself what you truly hunger for. Find what brings you joy
from within, and follow it. Only a soul enriched with meaning and contentment will make life feel
like a warm spring breeze, filled with genuine peace.
