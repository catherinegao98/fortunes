Fortune 33 (Moderately Fortunate)

Tiny details are like dust motes—accumulate enough and they form a mountain. This fortune
reminds you not to overlook the seemingly insignificant things. Be prudent and thorough in all
matters so you can nip potential problems in the bud, lest a small mistake snowball into a great
error. A cautious, meticulous approach will safeguard your journey, ensuring that you avoid pitfalls.
By minding the little things, you arm yourself with an unbeatable advantage and will remain secure,
even in the face of competition.
