Fortune 38 (Moderately Fortunate)

The past drifts away like smoke—do not lose yourself gazing backward. This fortune guides you to
lay aside lingering regrets and attachments to yesterday, and instead turn your eyes to the present
and future. The mistakes and sorrows behind you were lessons, no more. Learn what you can from
them, then let them go; there is no need to burden yourself with endless remorse. A new chapter of
life is already unfolding before you. Step forward unencumbered by old shadows, and embrace the
fresh hope glowing in the morning light.
