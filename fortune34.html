Fortune 34 (Very Fortunate)

The long night is ending—dawn’s light is on the horizon. This fortune brings a message of hope,
indicating that your troubles will disperse at last and a fortunate turn is near at hand. The toil and
persistence you invested in the past are now ripening into the fruit of luck. Just as the morning star
appears in the sky to guide a lost traveller, you will find your own guiding light and see your wish
fulfilled after enduring hardship. Hold onto your faith, for a new dawn is quietly breaking and with
it comes the fulfillment of your heart’s desire.
