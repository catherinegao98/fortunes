Fortune 46 (Very Fortunate)

A violet dawn rises, and all things align in your favour. This fortune is a sign of all-encompassing
good luck—you will find that in career, love, health, every facet of life flows smoothly and
harmoniously. A lucky star shines high above; all that you ask for is met with response. You’ll be
pleasantly surprised as obstacles dissolve effortlessly and your plans advance without hindrance. In
this time of abundant fortune, take the opportunity to expand your aspirations—your efforts now
will be rewarded twice over. Cherish this season of luck and remain grateful in heart, and the
blessings will linger long.
