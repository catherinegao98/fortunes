Fortune 1 (Moderately Fortunate)

In the words of ancient wisdom: "Heaven moves tirelessly, thus the noble soul perseveres without
rest." Your destiny unfolds like a steadfast steed on solid ground. Even if the road ahead is long, you
must continue forward with unyielding spirit. Results may not yet be visible, but each step you take
is quietly gathering power. Stay true to your original purpose and strive without ceasing—
eventually the clouds will part, the mist will clear, and a radiant dawn will rise for you.
