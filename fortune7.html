Fortune 7 (Very Fortunate)

A flame of ambition is kindled in your chest, illuminating a bright path for your career. This fortune
shows your talents will be fully unleashed, like a mighty dragon given wind and rain to soar into the
sky. Opportunities abound—seize them boldly, for each spark of passion will transform into
tangible achievement. Benefactors and superiors will lend their aid; your reputation and success will
rise with the tide. In the end, your grand aspirations will blossom and bear fruit.
