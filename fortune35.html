Fortune 35 (Moderately Fortunate)

All things seek balance under heaven. This fortune teaches that everything requires the middle path
—do not let any one aspect of life fall out of harmony. Work and rest are like a bird’s two wings:
without both, you cannot fly. Emotion and reason are two primal forces of life; only by blending
them in equilibrium can your life thrive without end. Embrace the middle way in all things. If you
maintain this balance, your fortunes will turn steadily and reliably like the cycle of seasons, and
your life will be blessed with greater harmony and well-being.
