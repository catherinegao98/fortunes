Fortune 8 (Moderately Fortunate)

The Wheel of Fortune turns slowly; patience is required. The situation now is like seeds planted in
winter, quietly awaiting spring’s awakening. Progress may seem slow, but hidden currents are
gathering strength beneath the surface. Do not become anxious or force matters—remember, fruit
falls naturally when it is ripe. Maintain your confidence and composure. When the opportune
moment arrives, your wish will sprout forth like a spring shoot breaking through the soil, ready to
grow.
