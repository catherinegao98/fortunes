Fortune 21 (Moderately Fortunate)

A prized sword lies sheathed, its brilliance concealed for now. This fortune means that though your
abilities are exceptional, you remain quietly reserved, awaiting the perfect moment to astound the
world. Just as a blade may be honed for years without yet tasting battle, when the time comes your
impact will amaze everyone. Continue to patiently gather experience and knowledge. When the
stage that is meant for you appears, you will shine in full, blazing glory and rise above all the rest.
