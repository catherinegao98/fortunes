Fortune 19 (Very Fortunate)

A lucky star shines down, and a helping hand reaches out. This fortune foretells you will encounter
a chance for elevation—perhaps a mentor, elder or superior will step in to guide you, resolving your
dilemma with ease. Just as a hidden gem is eventually recognized by a discerning eye, your talents
will be noticed by someone with wisdom. When opportunity knocks, do not let excessive modesty
make you shrink back. Embrace the assistance offered and let it become the ladder that carries you
to great heights.
