Fortune 47 (Moderately Fortunate)

One tree alone does not make a forest; a single flame struggles to stay alight. This fortune reminds
you that it is best to seek help and collaboration rather than fight every battle alone. If you meet a
challenge now, be humble and ask for guidance or gather support from friends. Many minds and
hands working together can overcome what one cannot. Through teamwork you will achieve an
unexpected breakthrough, and in mutual help you will also earn sincere friendship and trust. By
uniting with others, your lone spark can grow into a bright, warming fire.
