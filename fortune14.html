Fortune 14 (Moderately Fortunate)

Opportunity stands before you, and courage is the key to unlock it. This fortune signifies a new
beginning is at hand, but you must step forward boldly, like the Fool taking his first leap of faith.
Nothing ventured, nothing gained. Only by leaving the safety of your comfort zone can you
broaden the map of your life. Overcome your hesitation, and you’ll find the road opening wide
ahead. In taking this brave step, you will rejoice in the transformation and growth that follow.
