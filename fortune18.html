Fortune 18 (Moderately Fortunate)

Inner demons act like shackles, but they bind only what you allow. Your heart may be entangled by
a certain fear or desire; this fortune points out that these chains are not real—they are forged by the
mind and can be cast off by your own will. With calm introspection, face your fears and sever the
unhealthy attachments. In stillness find your courage, and cut through those illusory chains. You
will discover the darkness giving way to light, and you will regain command of your own destiny.
