Fortune 31 (Moderately Fortunate)

Great towers rise high, but beware a weak foundation. This fortune warns that when your career or
life achieves success, you must hold fast to humility. Do not indulge in boasting or arrogance—
pride builds a castle on sand that will surely collapse. Remember the old lesson: pride goes before
a fall, humility brings reward. Keep a modest heart and treat your good fortune as a passing gift.
By staying humble and grounded, your run of luck will endure, and success will remain steady
beneath your feet.
